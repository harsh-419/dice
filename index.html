Dice Game  

1. The game has 2 players, playing turnwise.

2. Initially the winning score is 100 but players can change upto their choice.

3. In a round player can roll as many times as he wants and scores get added to current score each time.

4. But if he rolls a 1, all his round score come to zero and it's next player's turn.

5. Player can choose to hold the current round score and it gets added to Global score, and then its turn for next player.
